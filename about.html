Our Story

Built by Students Who Lived the Process.

28E 77N was founded by former international students who experienced the confusion, anxiety, and excitement of applying to top universities abroad — and decided to build the service they wished they'd had.

How We Started

The Coordinates That Started It All

The name 28E 77N refers to the coordinates of New Delhi — where our founding team grew up, studied, and dreamed of universities on the other side of the world. When we applied to Oxford and Columbia more than a decade ago, we did it largely on our own: with imperfect information, well-meaning but uninformed guidance from school counselors, and a lot of guesswork.

We got in. But we knew how close we came to not getting in — and how much better it could have gone with real, experienced guidance. When we started working in and around admissions — as officers, as tutors, as graduate students — we realized the information gap hadn't closed. Ambitious students from across South Asia, the Middle East, and Southeast Asia were still navigating the most consequential application of their lives with inadequate support.

So in 2018, we started 28E 77N to fix that. We set out to build something boutique, honest, and genuinely effective: a small team of exceptional counselors who care more about outcomes than headcount, and who measure success not in application volumes but in students who arrive at their right-fit university ready to thrive.

What We Believe

Our Mission & Philosophy

We believe every student has a story worth telling — and that the right guidance doesn't fabricate that story, it reveals it.

🔍

Authenticity First

We never write essays for students or fabricate experiences. Our job is to help you articulate who you genuinely are — powerfully, precisely, and honestly.

🤝

Genuine Partnership

We work with a small number of students each cycle intentionally, so every family receives our full attention — not a generic process.

📐

Strategic Rigor

Good intentions aren't enough. We pair warmth with a data-driven, school-specific strategy built on years of outcomes data and first-hand institutional knowledge.

🌱

Long-Term Thinking

We help students find their right-fit university — not just any top-ranked school. Because the best outcome is a student who thrives, not just one who gets in.

"Our goal is not to get students into any university. It is to help them find the university where they will become the fullest version of themselves — and then help them get there."

— Priya Sharma, Co-Founder & Head of US Admissions

The Team

Your Counselors

Each member of our team has walked the path our students are on — as international students, as university employees, or both. Their expertise isn't theoretical. It's lived.
