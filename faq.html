Frequently Asked Questions

Honest Answers to Real Questions.

We'd rather you understand our process clearly before you commit to anything. Here are the questions we hear most often — answered directly.

Our Process

How We Work

The honest answer: earlier than you think, but it's never too late. For students targeting the most selective schools, starting in Grade 10 or 11 gives us the most room to help you with profile building, school research, and early positioning. If you're already in Grade 12, we can absolutely help — and we've had great results with students who came to us in September of their application year. Even in that scenario, there's meaningful work to do on essays, school list, and strategy.

For graduate school applicants, 12–18 months before your intended enrollment date is ideal.

Every engagement begins with a free 30-minute consultation where we learn about the student's goals, academic background, and timeline. From there, if it's a good fit, we move into a paid engagement with the following stages:

  1. Deep Assessment — A comprehensive review of your profile, transcripts, activities, and goals (typically 2 sessions).
  2. School List & Strategy — We build your college list and define the positioning strategy for each school.
  3. Essay Development — Brainstorming, drafting, and revision across all required essays.
  4. Application Assembly — Supporting you through activities lists, short answers, recommendations, and final submission.
  5. Decision Support — Interview prep, waitlist strategy, and final decision guidance.

After your free consultation, we match you based on your target destinations (US vs UK), the type of service you need (undergraduate, graduate, athletic, etc.), and the counselor whose expertise most closely aligns with your goals and background. We'll always explain who we're recommending and why — and if there's a fit issue after you start, we'll make adjustments.

We deliberately cap each counselor's active caseload during application season. This is central to how we deliver quality. Because we keep numbers small, your counselor knows your application inside out — not just your name and target schools, but your writing style, your anxieties, your family situation, and what kind of feedback works for you.

This is also why we sometimes have a waitlist during peak periods (summer through November). If you're worried about timing, reach out early.
