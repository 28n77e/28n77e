Our Services

A Service for Every Stage of Your Journey

Whether you're a Year 10 student just starting to think about university, or a final-year applicant making the last push — we have the right support for where you are right now.

Jump to: Admissions Counseling Essay Mentoring Profile Building Test Prep Athletic Recruitment Graduate School
01 Our Core Offering

College Admissions Counseling

Our flagship service guides students through the entire application cycle — from initial self-discovery and college list building to final submission, waitlist strategy, and decision-making. We don't hand you a checklist; we become your dedicated partner.

👤 Who it's for: High school students in Grades 9–12 applying to universities in the US, UK, or both
What's included
  • Deep-dive student assessment and profile analysis
  • Personalized college list construction (reach, match, safety)
  • School-specific positioning strategy
  • Application timeline management and deadline tracking
  • Common App, UCAS, and Coalition App guidance
  • Interview preparation (mock sessions + feedback)
  • Financial aid and scholarship strategy
  • Waitlist, deferral, and decision support
Start with a Free Consultation
02 Find Your Voice

Essay Mentoring & Creative Writing Support

The personal essay is the one place in the application where you can step off the page and say: this is who I am. Our essay mentors — many of them published writers and former admissions readers — help you find that voice and use it with precision.

👤 Who it's for: Any student who wants to make their essays the strongest part of their application
What's included
  • Brainstorming sessions to surface the right story
  • Structural outlining and narrative arc development
  • Up to 10 rounds of detailed, tracked revisions
  • Common App main essay (650 words)
  • All supplemental essays (Why This School, Activities, etc.)
  • UCAS personal statement (4,000 characters)
  • Copyediting and final polish pass
Start with a Free Consultation
03 Long-Term Strategy

Profile & Extracurricular Building

The strongest applicants don't just have long activity lists — they have a coherent story. This service is for students in Grades 9–11 who want to be intentional about what they pursue, why they pursue it, and how it adds up to something that admissions committees find genuinely compelling.

👤 Who it's for: Students in Grades 9–11 who want to start building a strategic, authentic application profile
What's included
  • Strengths, interests, and passions mapping
  • Extracurricular strategy and prioritization
  • Leadership opportunity identification and planning
  • Research, competition, and independent project guidance
  • Summer program and internship advising
  • Activities list crafting and narrative framing
  • Annual check-in sessions (typically 3–4 per year)
Start with a Free Consultation
04 Test Strategy

Test Prep Guidance

We don't run test prep classes — we help you make smart decisions about tests: which one to take, when to take it, when scores add value versus when test-optional is the right call, and how to structure your prep to maximize your score within your schedule.

👤 Who it's for: Students preparing for the SAT, ACT, IELTS, TOEFL, or AP exams as part of their international university application
What's included
  • Diagnostic assessment and test-choice recommendation
  • Customized prep plan and study schedule
  • Referrals to our vetted test prep partner network
  • Score analysis and submission strategy
  • Test-optional decision support (by school)
  • AP/IB exam selection guidance
Start with a Free Consultation
05 For Student-Athletes

Athletic Recruitment Consulting

Being a competitive athlete opens additional pathways into highly selective schools — but only if you navigate the process correctly and early. Our athletic recruitment specialists help you communicate with coaches, manage timelines, and integrate your athletic profile with your academic application.

👤 Who it's for: Student-athletes competing at a high level who want to pursue college athletics alongside top-tier academics
What's included
  • Athletic profile and highlight reel review
  • NCAA/NAIA eligibility center guidance
  • Coach outreach strategy and email templates
  • School list building across athletic and academic fit
  • Official visit preparation
  • Coordination of academic and athletic application timelines
Start with a Free Consultation
06 Beyond the Undergrad

Graduate School Admissions

The graduate admissions game is different — more competitive, more specialized, and more reliant on how you position your professional story. Whether you're targeting a top MBA, law school, or medical program, our graduate specialists bring deep school-specific expertise and a results-driven approach.

👤 Who it's for: Undergraduate students and young professionals applying to MBA, JD, MD, or other graduate and professional programs
What's included
  • Program research and school list development
  • GMAT, GRE, LSAT, and MCAT strategy guidance
  • Resume and professional narrative crafting
  • Statement of purpose and personal statement coaching
  • Recommendation letter strategy and reviewer coaching
  • Interview preparation (behavioral + school-specific)
  • Waitlist and reapplication strategy
